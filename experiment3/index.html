Experiment 03 - Alternate Worlds


Description

This project is actually 2 separate p5.js projects that are making a dungeon and overworld generator respectively. The dungeon generator has a moving vignette for a creepy effect while the overworld generator has clouds moving across the screen as if the user was looking at the biome from top-down. The dungeon generator has a snowy and barren aesthetic with a dark dungeon between the snow with paths connecting them. There are also dungeon towers in the snow. The overworld generator has ponds and rocks scattered around the biome as well as clusters of trees and some homes littered around too.

Technical

This project is made with an ascii grid that is representing the positions of tiles placed at different ascii values.
